# Apply to the Tiresias Partner Program

Join the first wave of partners selling purpose-built AI agent security. Whether you are a security consultancy, an MSSP, an AI platform company, or a system integrator, there is a place for you in the Tiresias Partner Program.

---

## What We Are Looking For

We partner with organizations that have existing relationships with enterprise security and engineering teams. The strongest partners typically fall into one of these categories:

- **Security consultants and advisory firms** who advise on identity, access management, and compliance
- **Managed Security Service Providers (MSSPs)** who deliver ongoing security operations for their clients
- **AI platform companies** whose customers are building or deploying autonomous agents
- **System integrators** who implement security infrastructure for mid-market and enterprise organizations

Prior AI experience is not required. We provide comprehensive training materials and sales support to get you productive quickly.

---

## What You Get

- **25% recurring rev-share** on every dollar of MRR your customers generate, for as long as they remain on the platform (configurable 10% to 40% based on volume)
- **Complete sales kit** including product datasheets, competitive battlecard, pricing reference, and sales playbook
- **Partner portal access** with real-time MRR tracking, tenant management, and payout history
- **Dedicated partner support** via email and Slack, with direct access to the Tiresias team for deal support and technical questions
- **White-label options** for MSSP partners, including custom branding, logo, and CSS for sub-tenant portals

## What Happens Next

1. **Confirmation.** You will receive an email confirming your application was received.
2. **Review.** The Tiresias partnerships team reviews every application within **2 business days**. We may reach out with clarifying questions.
3. **Approval.** Upon approval, you receive your partner portal credentials, API soulkey, unique partner code, and onboarding documentation in a single welcome email.
4. **Onboarding.** Follow the Partner Onboarding Guide to set up your account, configure billing, and make your first sale.

Questions before applying? Contact **partners@saluca.com**.
