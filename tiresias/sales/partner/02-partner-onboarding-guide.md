**Welcome to the Tiresias Partner Program**

This guide walks you through everything you need to go from newly approved partner to making your first sale. Whether you are a Reseller or an MSSP Partner, follow these phases in order and you will be fully operational within days.

## Phase 1: Application and Approval

### Step 1: Submit Your Application

You can apply through either of two channels:

1. **Portal form** -- Visit [tiresias.network/partners/apply](https://tiresias.network/partners/apply) and complete the application form.
2. **API** -- Submit a `POST` request to `/v1/partners/apply` with the required fields (see below).

**Required information:**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Company name | Your legal entity name |
| Company domain | Your primary website domain |
| Contact name | Primary contact for the partnership |
| Contact email | Must be on your company domain |
| Partner type | `reseller` or `mssp` |
| Expected monthly volume | Number of customers you expect to onboard in the first 12 months |

**Optional but helpful:**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Contact phone | Direct line for your primary contact |
| Existing customer base | Current managed customers (especially relevant for MSSP applicants) |
| Company description | What you do and why you want to partner with Tiresias |
| Referral source | How you heard about us |

**API example:**

```bash
curl -X POST https://tiresias.network/v1/partners/apply \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "company_name": "Acme Security Inc.",
    "company_domain": "acmesec.com",
    "contact_name": "Jane Smith",
    "contact_email": "jane@acmesec.com",
    "partner_type": "mssp",
    "expected_monthly_volume": 20,
    "existing_customer_base": 50,
    "company_description": "Managed SOC provider serving mid-market enterprises"
  }'
```

You will receive a `202 Accepted` response with your application ID and a confirmation that review is underway.

### Step 2: Wait for Review

- Applications are reviewed by the Saluca partnerships team within **2 business days**.
- Our team receives a notification in Slack and email the moment your application arrives.
- There is nothing you need to do during this period. We may reach out with clarifying questions.

### Step 3: Approval and Credential Issuance

When your application is approved, the system automatically provisions the following:

| What You Receive | Description |
|---|---|
| Partner tenant | An MSSP-tier tenant (for MSSP partners) or Pro-tier tenant (for Resellers) |
| Admin soulkey | Your API key for authenticating to all partner endpoints |
| License JWT | Your platform license with your `partner_id` claim embedded |
| Partner code | A unique code (e.g., `ACME-SEC-2026`) used in referral URLs and tracking |
| Rev-share configuration | Your rev-share percentage, billing model, and payout frequency |
| Portal access | Login credentials for the Partner Dashboard at tiresias.network |

All of this arrives in a single welcome email. Save your admin soulkey immediately; it is shown once and cannot be retrieved later.

## Phase 2: Setting Up Your Partner Account

### Accessing the Partner Portal

Log in to [tiresias.network](https://tiresias.network) with the credentials from your welcome email. Your partner dashboard is at:

- **Dashboard overview:** `/dashboard/partner`
- **Tenant/customer management:** `/dashboard/partner/tenants`
- **Billing and payouts:** `/dashboard/partner/billing`
- **Settings:** `/dashboard/partner/settings`

### Understanding Your Dashboard

The dashboard overview (`/dashboard/partner`) shows your key metrics at a glance:

| Metric | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Active tenants | Number of customer tenants currently active under your partnership |
| Total agents | Sum of all agents registered across your customer tenants |
| Current month MRR | Total monthly recurring revenue generated by your customers this period |
| Rev-share earned (this month) | Your earnings for the current billing period |
| Lifetime earnings | Cumulative rev-share earned since your partnership began |
| Pending payouts | Earned amounts not yet disbursed |

The dashboard also displays a recent activity feed showing tenant provisioning events, payout processing, and tier changes.

### For MSSP Partners: Creating Sub-Tenants

As an MSSP partner, you provision and manage tenants on behalf of your customers. You can do this through the portal or the API.

**Through the portal:**

1. Navigate to `/dashboard/partner/tenants`.
2. Click "New Tenant."
3. Fill in the tenant name, slug, tier, and admin email for your customer.
4. Click create.

**Through the API:**

```bash
curl -X POST https://tiresias.network/v1/partners/tenants \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_SOULKEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "name": "Customer Corp",
    "slug": "customer-corp",
    "tier": "pro",
    "admin_email": "admin@customercorp.com"
  }'
```

**Tier constraints you need to know:**

- You can create sub-tenants at these tiers: **Open, Starter, Pro, Enterprise**.
- You **cannot** create sub-tenants at the MSSP or SaaS tier. This is enforced server-side; any attempt returns a `422` error.
- Sub-tenants are flat. Your customers cannot create their own sub-tenants. The maximum hierarchy depth is **1 level** below your partner root.
- Your customers see their tenant as a standalone Tiresias deployment. They have no visibility into the partner hierarchy.

### For Resellers: How Referral Tracking Works

Resellers do not provision tenants directly. Instead, you refer customers using your unique partner code.

1. Navigate to `/dashboard/partner/tenants` (labeled "Customers" for resellers).
2. Copy your referral link. It follows the format: `tiresias.network/?ref=YOUR_PARTNER_CODE`
3. Share this link with prospects. When they sign up through it, their tenant is automatically tagged with your `partner_id`.
4. You can see all referred customers, their tier, MRR, and signup date in your dashboard.

You cannot modify your customers' tenants. They manage their own accounts, choose their own tier, and handle their own billing. You earn rev-share on their subscription MRR.

### Setting Up Billing

**Phase 1 operates on the Partner-Billed model.** Here is how it works:

- **You** are the Stripe customer. Your MSSP subscription ($4,999/mo base + $199 per sub-tenant) is billed to your payment method.
- Sub-tenant usage is metered automatically. Each time you provision a new tenant, the metered count increments on your subscription.
- **You bill your own clients** however you choose. Tiresias does not have a direct billing relationship with your sub-tenants in this model.
- A monthly reconciliation process verifies that metered billing matches your actual tenant count.

For Resellers: your own tenant subscription is billed normally. Your rev-share earnings accrue separately based on your referred customers' MRR.

### White-Label Options (MSSP Only)

MSSP partners can customize the portal experience for their customers. Navigate to `/dashboard/partner/settings` to configure:

| Option | Description |
|---|---|
| Custom CSS | A stylesheet URL or inline CSS applied to the sub-tenant portal |
| Logo URL | Your company logo, displayed in place of the Tiresias logo for your sub-tenants |
| Favicon URL | Custom browser tab icon |
| Display name | Your company name shown to sub-tenant users (e.g., "Powered by Acme Security") |

Use the preview button to verify your branding before publishing. Reseller partners do not have access to white-label features.

### Key Discovery Questions

Use these questions to qualify prospects and identify their pain points. They are drawn from the Tiresias sales playbook and cover the scenarios that most reliably lead to conversion.

1. **"How many AI agents are you running in production today? How do you expect that to grow?"**
   Establishes scale. Prospects running 10+ agents in production are ideal.

2. **"How do your agents authenticate to each other and to external services?"**
   Surfaces identity gaps. Most teams rely on static API keys with no rotation or scoping.

3. **"If an agent was compromised today, how would you know? How quickly could you isolate it?"**
   Reveals detection and response gaps. This is where SoulWatch and quarantine capabilities resonate.

4. **"How are you managing API keys across your agent fleet? Who has access to rotate them?"**
   Highlights operational risk. Key sprawl is a universal pain point.

5. **"What does your agent authorization model look like? Standing permissions, or something more dynamic?"**
   Opens the door to SoulAuth's just-in-time authorization and capability tokens.

6. **"What compliance frameworks are you subject to? SOC2, ISO, NIST?"**
   Compliance-driven buyers convert faster and at higher tiers (Pro or Enterprise).

7. **"Is privacy a concern with your current agent security tools? Do they inspect payloads?"**
   Sets up the zero-knowledge architecture differentiator. Tiresias sees threats, never data.

### The 15-Minute Demo Path

The fastest way to demonstrate Tiresias value is a live deployment. The entire platform runs via Docker Compose and can be up in under 15 minutes.

**Step 1: Deploy the stack.**
Run Docker Compose to bring up SoulAuth, SoulWatch, SoulGate, and the Caddy reverse proxy. The prospect sees the platform starting from zero.

**Step 2: Register an agent.**
Create a soulkey identity for a test agent. Show how the agent receives a cryptographic identity (SHA-512 hash) without Tiresias ever seeing the agent's payloads.

**Step 3: Show SoulAuth policy.**
Define a YAML authorization policy. Demonstrate just-in-time policy evaluation: the agent requests access, SoulAuth evaluates the policy, and a short-lived capability token is issued.

**Step 4: Trigger a SoulWatch detection.**
Generate anomalous behavior (unusual call frequency, out-of-pattern timing). Show SoulWatch detecting the anomaly in real time, scoring the risk, and firing an alert.

**Step 5: Show SoulGate blocking.**
Send a request containing a prompt injection pattern through SoulGate. Show the 7-step security pipeline catching and blocking the request. The prospect sees the blocked request in the audit log.

This sequence hits all three products and demonstrates the full security lifecycle: identity, monitoring, and enforcement.

### Trial-to-Paid Conversion

After the 14-day trial, your prospect will either convert to a paid tier or drop to Open. Here is how to support the conversion:

- **Day 1 to 3:** Help them deploy and register their first agents. The faster they see their own data in the dashboard, the stickier the trial becomes.
- **Day 4 to 7:** Walk them through SoulWatch detections and SoulAuth policies using their real agent fleet. This is when value becomes concrete.
- **Day 8 to 10:** Discuss tier selection. Use the pricing reference to match their agent count and compliance needs to the right tier.
- **Day 11 to 14:** Confirm the purchase decision. Handle any remaining objections. Coordinate with their procurement team if needed.
- **After Day 14:** If they have not converted, they automatically move to Open tier. They retain access but lose Pro features (Sigma rules, SIEM integration, quarantine management, RBAC). Follow up within a week; the feature gap often drives conversion.

## Phase 4: Managing Your Business

### Reading Your Rev-Share Reports

Rev-share is calculated on the 1st of each month at 00:00 UTC. The calculation works as follows:

1. The system queries all your sub-tenants (MSSP) or referred customers (Reseller).
2. For each, it fetches the Stripe subscription MRR.
3. Your earnings are calculated: `sub-tenant MRR x your rev-share percentage`.
4. The rev-share percentage is **snapshotted at calculation time**, so any mid-month changes to your rate take effect the following period.
5. All amounts are summed across your customer base.

Access your reports at `/dashboard/partner/billing`. The current period section shows accruing earnings in real time, and the payout history table shows all past periods.

### Payout Mechanics

| Parameter | Default | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Payout frequency | Monthly | Can be set to quarterly at approval time |
| Minimum threshold | $50 | Earnings below this amount roll over to the next period |
| Rev-share percentage | 25% | Configurable per partner (range: 10% to 40%) |

**Phase 1 (current):** Payouts are processed manually by the Saluca finance team after the monthly reconciliation. You will receive an email when your payout is processed.

**Phase 2 (upcoming):** Payouts will be automated via Stripe Connect. You will onboard your bank account through the partner settings page, and transfers will be created automatically.

### Audit Log Access

All partner operations are logged to an immutable audit trail. This includes:

- Tenant creation, suspension, and reactivation events
- Configuration changes (rev-share, white-label settings)
- API key rotations
- Payout events
- Failed operations (such as attempts to create sub-tenants with blocked tiers)

Each log entry captures the actor (soulkey), action, target, details, IP address, and timestamp. This data is available for your own compliance reporting needs.

### Scaling Your Business

**Adding more customers:**
- MSSP partners: provision new sub-tenants through the portal or API as described in Phase 2 of this guide. Your metered billing adjusts automatically.
- Resellers: share your referral link with new prospects. No provisioning steps needed on your end.

**Upgrading customer tiers:**
- Sub-tenants can be upgraded from Open through Enterprise at any time.
- Upgrades to MSSP or SaaS tier are not permitted for partner sub-tenants. The maximum tier for any customer under a partner is Enterprise.
- Tier upgrades can be initiated through the portal or the Stripe subscription management flow.
