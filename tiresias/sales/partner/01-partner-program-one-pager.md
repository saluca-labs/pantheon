## The Opportunity

AI agents are the fastest-growing attack surface in enterprise IT. 73% of enterprises now deploy autonomous AI agents in production, yet virtually none govern them. Every agent is an identity without oversight, calling APIs, accessing data stores, and communicating with other agents on implicit trust.

This is a greenfield market. Traditional IAM vendors were not built for this. Agent wrapper tools only protect individual agents, not fleets. No one has shipped a purpose-built platform for AI agent security at the infrastructure level.

Tiresias is first. And we are looking for sales engineers who want to own this category with us.

## What You Sell

**Tiresias** is a zero-knowledge security platform for AI agent infrastructure. Three products, one platform:

| Product | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **SoulAuth** | Agent identity and zero-trust authorization. Cryptographic soulkeys, just-in-time permissions, policy-as-code synced from git. |
| **SoulWatch** | Runtime security monitoring. Behavioral anomaly detection, Sigma-compatible rules, SIEM integration (Splunk, Elastic, Sentinel). |
| **SoulGate** | API security gateway. Rate limiting, prompt injection detection (36 patterns), circuit breakers, threat detection at the API layer. |

Customers can buy products individually or as a **Platform Bundle** at a discount.

**Friction-free entry:** 14-day free trial, full Pro access, no credit card required, deploys in under 15 minutes via Docker Compose.

## Two Partner Models

### Reseller

Refer customers to Tiresias using your unique partner link. Customers sign up, choose their own tier, and manage their own accounts. You earn a recurring rev-share on every dollar of MRR they generate, for as long as they remain a customer.

- No tenant management required
- Customers handle their own billing and configuration
- You focus on introductions and closing; Tiresias handles everything else

### MSSP Partner

Operate a managed security practice on top of Tiresias. Provision and manage sub-tenants for your end customers directly from the partner portal. Set your own pricing, apply your own branding, and deliver agent security as a managed service.

- Create and manage sub-tenants (Starter, Pro, or Enterprise tier)
- White-label branding (custom logo, CSS, display name)
- Full tenant lifecycle management via portal and API
- Bill customers directly at your own margins, or earn rev-share through Tiresias billing
