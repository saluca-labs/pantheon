## Two Partner Models

### Reseller

Refer customers to Tiresias using your unique partner link. Customers sign up, choose their own tier, and manage their own accounts. You earn recurring rev-share on their subscription MRR with no tenant management required.

- No provisioning or configuration needed on your end
- Customers handle their own billing and account management
- You focus on introductions and closing; Tiresias handles everything else
- Ideal for security consultants, advisory firms, and technology advisors

**Example:** 10 customers on Pro ($199/mo each) = $1,990 MRR. Your 25% share = **$497.50/mo**.

### MSSP Partner

Operate a managed security practice on top of Tiresias. Provision and manage sub-tenants for your end customers directly from the partner portal. Set your own pricing, apply your own branding, and deliver agent security as a managed service.

- Create and manage sub-tenants at Community, Starter, Pro, or Enterprise tier
- White-label branding with custom logo, CSS, favicon, and display name
- Full tenant lifecycle management via portal and API
- Bill customers directly at your own margins, or earn rev-share through Tiresias billing

**Example:** 10 sub-tenants on Enterprise ($2,499/mo each) = $24,990 MRR. Your 25% share = **$6,247.50/mo**.
