# Partner Program FAQ

Answers to the most common questions from prospective and current Tiresias partners. For detailed information, refer to the linked resources or contact partners@saluca.com.

---

### 1. How much can I earn?

Your earnings depend on how many customers you bring to the platform and which tiers they subscribe to. As a reference point, 10 customers on Pro ($199/mo each) earns you $497.50/mo at the default 25% rev-share. 10 customers on Enterprise ($2,499/mo each) earns $6,247.50/mo. Revenue is recurring for as long as your customers remain active. See the [Partner Program One-Pager](/partners/docs/partner-one-pager.pdf) for detailed scenarios.

### 2. How does the rev-share work?

Rev-share is calculated on the 1st of each month based on your customers' active Stripe subscription MRR. Your percentage (default 25%, configurable from 10% to 40%) is applied to each customer's MRR and summed across your entire portfolio. Earnings accrue in real time and are visible in your partner dashboard. See the [Onboarding Guide](/partners/docs/onboarding-guide) for full details on calculation and payout mechanics.

### 3. What is the difference between Reseller and MSSP?

Resellers refer customers using a unique partner link. Customers sign up, manage their own accounts, and handle their own billing. You earn rev-share with no operational overhead. MSSP partners provision and manage sub-tenants on behalf of their customers, with white-label branding, custom pricing, and full tenant lifecycle control. MSSPs can bill customers directly or use Tiresias billing with rev-share.

### 4. Is there a fee to join?

No. There is no application fee, no annual fee, and no minimum commitment. Resellers receive a Pro-tier tenant at no cost. MSSP partners subscribe to the MSSP tier ($4,999/mo base + $199 per sub-tenant), which provides the infrastructure to operate a managed practice.

### 5. How do I get paid?

Payouts are currently processed monthly by the Saluca finance team after reconciliation. You receive an email when your payout is processed. The minimum payout threshold is $50; amounts below this roll over to the next period. Automated payouts via Stripe Connect are coming soon, at which point transfers will be created automatically to your connected bank account.

### 6. What sales support do you provide?

Every partner receives a complete sales toolkit: product datasheets for SoulAuth, SoulWatch, and SoulGate; a competitive battlecard; a pricing reference; a sales playbook with discovery questions and demo scripts; and a technical architecture brief. You also get direct access to the Tiresias team through email and the #partner-ops Slack channel for deal support, technical questions, and co-selling on larger opportunities.

### 7. Can I white-label Tiresias?

Yes, but only for MSSP partners. You can customize the portal experience your customers see with your own logo, CSS, favicon, and display name. Your sub-tenants see your branding, not Tiresias branding. Resellers do not have access to white-label features. See the white-label configuration section of the [Onboarding Guide](/partners/docs/onboarding-guide).

### 8. What does Tiresias actually do?

Tiresias is a zero-knowledge security platform for AI agent infrastructure. It provides three products: SoulAuth (agent identity and zero-trust authorization), SoulWatch (runtime behavioral monitoring and anomaly detection), and SoulGate (API security gateway with prompt injection detection). Together, they give enterprises identity, monitoring, and enforcement for their entire agent fleet without ever seeing the agents' data.

### 9. Who are the target customers?

Any organization running AI agents in production. The strongest buyers are mid-market and enterprise companies with 10 or more agents deployed, especially those subject to compliance frameworks like SOC2, ISO 27001, or NIST. Security teams, DevOps teams, and platform engineering teams are the typical buyers. Compliance-driven organizations convert faster and at higher tiers.

### 10. How long is the sales cycle?

Most deals close within 2 to 4 weeks. Tiresias offers a 14-day free trial with full Pro access and no credit card required. The platform deploys in under 15 minutes via Docker Compose, so prospects can evaluate it in a single afternoon. The trial period is typically sufficient for the prospect to see value with their own agent fleet. Enterprise deals with procurement involvement may take longer.

### 11. What happens when my customer needs support?

For Reseller customers, Tiresias provides direct support according to the customer's tier SLA (Open: community forums; Starter: 48-hour email; Pro: 24-hour priority; Enterprise: 8-hour dedicated). For MSSP sub-tenants, you are the first line of support. You can escalate to Tiresias through the partner support channels when needed.

### 12. Can I sell individual products (just SoulAuth)?

Yes. Customers can purchase SoulAuth, SoulWatch, or SoulGate individually, or buy the Platform Bundle at a discount. This flexibility makes it easier to land initial deals; you can start with the product that addresses the prospect's most immediate pain point and expand to the full platform over time.

### 13. What is the contract term?

Customers can subscribe on a monthly or annual basis. Annual billing saves 17% (2 months free) and locks in 12 months of predictable revenue for both you and your customer. There is no long-term contract required for the partner program itself; you can participate for as long as you choose.

### 14. How do I track my referrals and commissions?

Everything is visible in the partner dashboard at /dashboard/partner. You can see all referred customers (Reseller) or managed sub-tenants (MSSP), their tier, MRR, signup date, and your rev-share earnings in real time. The billing section provides historical payout records and exportable CSV reports for your own accounting.

### 15. What if I want to upgrade from Reseller to MSSP?

Contact partners@saluca.com to request an upgrade. The transition involves provisioning an MSSP-tier tenant and migrating your partner configuration. Your existing referral customers and earned rev-share are preserved. The main change is that you gain the ability to provision and manage sub-tenants, apply white-label branding, and bill customers directly.
